Who I am

Hi! I'm Melvin Shaju, based in Adelaide, Australia — currently completing my Master's in AI & Machine Learning at Adelaide University, with a background in Mechatronics Engineering (Anna University).

My background gives me a rare edge — I understand both the physical world of sensors, motors, and control systems and the intelligence layer of neural networks, computer vision, and ML models. Most AI engineers can't read a circuit. Most hardware engineers can't train a model. I can do both.

I'm focused on applying this combination to Healthcare AI (smart patient monitoring, medical imaging, hospital automation), Robotics (autonomous systems, perception, motion planning), Intelligent Automation, and AI Software — building intelligent apps, LLM-powered tools, and computer vision products that bring AI to real users and businesses.

I also have real hands-on care experience at Altaira — which means I understand the human side of healthcare technology, not just the technical side. I believe the best healthcare AI is built by people who truly understand patients.

And above everything — Jesus is the foundation of everything I do. My faith gives me the strength to dream big, the courage to keep going when things are hard, and the heart to genuinely care for others. Every project, every goal, every step forward — it's all purposeful. ✝️
